About Us

Welcome to Our Haven of Healing

At Shree Surgical Hospital, we believe that healthcare is more than just treating illnesses—it’s about nurturing the human spirit. Nestled in the heart of Jalna, our hospital is a beacon of hope and a sanctuary of healing, where cutting-edge technology meets compassionate care.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide exceptional healthcare with a human touch at an affordable cost. We are dedicated to improve the lives of our patients through innovative treatments, holistic care, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Vision

We envision a world where healthcare is accessible, personalized, and empowering. Our goal is to be the healthcare provider of choice, renowned for our patient-centered approach and our relentless pursuit of medical advancements.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with kindness, empathy, and respect.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in medical care and patient service.
  • Innovation: We embrace cutting-edge technology and innovative practices to enhance patient outcomes.
  • Integrity: We conduct ourselves with honesty, transparency, and ethical integrity.

Our Team

Our team of highly skilled and dedicated professionals includes Orthopaedic Surgeons, Gynaecologists, ENT Surgeons, Radiologists and Pathologists who are passionate about providing personalized care. With diverse specialties and a collaborative spirit, our multidisciplinary team works together to create tailored treatment plans for each patient.

Patient-Centered Care

At our Hospital, you are not just a patient; you are part of our family. We take the time to listen, understand your needs, and involve you in your care plan. Our holistic approach addresses not only physical health but also emotional and mental well-being, ensuring comprehensive care for every individual.
